## Time and location

* <time>Sunday, March 22nd, 2026, 1:00pm–2:00pm</time>
* In-person event
* Location: Portland Art Museum, 1219 SW Park Ave, Portland, OR 97205; [RSVP](#rsvp) is required.

## About

This one-hour tour of the [Portland Art Museum](https://portlandartmuseum.org/){: rel="external"} will include a tour of accessibility features, followed by an exploration of the museum's tactile graphics.

The studio has been reserved - so we will have a quiet place to talk and explore the tactiles. Personal belongings can be left in this area during the tour, as the studio will be securely locked.

**This tour is being offered free of charge, but an RSVP is required.** Once the tour is completed, food and beverages can be purchased at [Coquelico](https://www.coquelicopdx.com/){: rel="external"} within the Portland Art Museum. Attendees can continue to tour the museum if they wish.

**Museum Staff Request:** Please avoid wearing scented body and laundry products, as well as essential oils.

**Accessibility Request:** The Portland Art Museum (PAM) now has elevators and an accessible entrance. Here are further details on the [PAM's accessibility offerings](https://portlandartmuseum.org/accessibility/){: rel="external"}. Please let us know if you have any accommodation requests, such as stools, etc.

**Where to Meet:** The lobby, more details to come. Please look for our logo.

## Accommodation requests

If you have any accommodation requests, don't hesitate to [contact us](/contact.html) before the event to discuss.
